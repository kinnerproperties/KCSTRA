Our Mission

Building a stronger, more unified short-term rental community in Kansas City.

The Voice for Legal Short-Term Rentals

KCSTRA is a group of residents, hosts, local businesses, and advocates who understand and protect the rights of homeowners to responsibly rent their properties. We believe in fair and reasonable regulations of short-term rentals in Kansas City, and know the value that private hosting brings to our area's economy.

We work to develop reasonable regulations and responsible management, serving as a resource and platform for our members with updates on policy proposals, relevant news, and opportunities for action.

Our mission is to balance a thriving short-term rental community which supports both property owners' options and neighborhoods — collaborating with and educating our community, public officials, STR owners/operators, and associated businesses.
